🌙 Zakat al-Fitr (Fitra)

About Fitra: Zakat al-Fitr is a compulsory charity paid by the head of household on behalf of themselves and every dependent, before the Eid al-Fitr prayer. It is equivalent to about 2 kg of staple food (wheat, barley, dates, rice) per person.
